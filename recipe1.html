Pumpkin Pie

by Anna Blood

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups pumpkin pie puree
  • 3/4 cup canned coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • little bit of orange (optional)
  • Pie crust

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into pie crust.
  3. Bake in oven at 350 degrees Farenheit for 60 minutes.
  4. Cool and refrigerate for 4 hours.
